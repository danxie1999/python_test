DearDan,
Greetings from Kuala Lumpur! We're in south-east Asia this week meeting with graduates at our international alumni receptions in Indonesia, Malaysia and Singapore <https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fevents%2finternational&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066>, while back in Sydney, it's a big week on campus as we offer a warm welcome to our newest alumni who are celebrating at graduations ceremonies.
[image: What's Ahead? UTS alumna Leonie Sii shares her graduation experience] <https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fyoutu.be%2f7i3EPzToTCs&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066>	

Do you remember that feeling? Equal parts excitement and trepidation as you heard your name called, took a deep breath and then walked across the stage to receive your testamur. For many of us, that moment is the pinnacle of many years of dedicated study – and truly a milestone. This week (and next), we welcome almost 4,000 graduates into our alumni community. We've invited this year's graduates to share their favourite graduations moments – take a look <https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.facebook.com%2fpg%2fUTSAlumni%2fphotos%2f%3ftab%3dalbum%26album_id%3d871475336335233&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066>.
We'd also love to hear where your journey has taken you since your time with us, what you're interested in, and what you'd like to see more of from us. If you could spare five minutes to complete our alumni questionnaire <https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fmy-uts%2falumni-survey&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066>, we would really appreciate it.
This June we'll be hosting alumni receptions throughout China <https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fevents%2finternational&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066> – and we're also heading to London with a special event for alumni hosted by the Vice-Chancellor on June 27 <https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fevents%2finternational%2f2017%2fvice-chancellors-london-alumni-reception&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066> – register your place now.
Our latest issue of Tower magazine is out this week <https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fnews%2ftower&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066>, and we hear from our new Chancellor Catherine Livingstone AO about her vision for UTS and her passion for innovation and technology <https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fnews%2ftower%2fissue-16%2fgetting-down-to-business&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066>. As one of Australia's most highly regarded business leaders, having helmed some of Australia's largest organisations – including the Commonwealth Bank, Telstra, the CSIRO and Cochlear – we are delighted to welcome her to UTS.
We encourage the innovators among you to apply for UTS's unique Hatchery+ initiative for alumni <https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fhatchery.uts.edu.au%2fhatchery-plus%2f&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066> – a three-month program providing budding alumni entrepreneurs with workspace, seed funding, masterclasses, mentorship from experts and invaluable connections with the Sydney and international start-up ecosystem. Learn about Annette McClelland's Hatchery+ experience and find out more about this fantastic free program <https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fnews%2f2017%2fhatchlings-take-flight&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066> and how to apply.

Engineering alumnus Greg McTaggart <https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fconnect%2falumni-profiles%2fgreg-mctaggart&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066> also shares his passion for sustainability and mega events in a career encompassing the 2000 Sydney Olympics and most recently, as the Director of Building for the iconic Sydney Opera House.
For those thinking of further study, applications for postgraduate enrolments are now open for second semester <https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.uts.edu.au%2ffuture-students%2fpostgraduate&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066>, with UTS alumni automatically entitled to a 10% saving on degree courses through the UTS Alumni Advantage <https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fadvantage&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066>. As one of Australia's most highly regarded business leaders, having helmed some of Australia's largest organisations &#8211; including the Commonwealth Bank, Telstra, the CSIRO and Cochlear &#8211; we are delighted to welcome her to UTS.</p>
<p>We encourage the innovators among you to apply for <a style="text-decoration: none; color: #0d2c6c;" href="https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fhatchery.uts.edu.au%2fhatchery-plus%2f&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066">UTS's unique Hatchery+ initiative for alumni</a> &#8211; a three-month program providing budding alumni entrepreneurs with workspace, seed funding, masterclasses, mentorship from experts and invaluable connections with the Sydney and international start-up ecosystem. <a style="text-decoration: none; color: #0d2c6c;" href="https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fnews%2f2017%2fhatchlings-take-flight&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066">Learn about Annette McClelland's Hatchery+ experience and find out more about this fantastic free program</a> and how to apply.</p>
<p>Get inspired by fellow graduates who have been motivated by their personal experiences to make a difference, including the remarkable<a style="text-decoration: none; color: #0d2c6c;" href="https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fnews%2ftower%2fissue-16%2fbetween-faith-and-football&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066"> Amna Karra-Hassan</a>, the Australian-Muslim woman from Western Sydney who is tackling the cultural barriers in the AFL; <a style="text-decoration: none; color: #0d2c6c;" href="https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fnews%2f2017%2fwhen-war-comes-home&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066">Ukrainian-born Anna Denejkina</a> who is researching how wartime experiences can affect a child's development; <a style="text-decoration: none; color: #0d2c6c;" href="https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=http%3a%2f%2fnewsroom.uts.edu.au%2fcontent%2flife-and-legacy&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066">Kate Fitzsimons</a>, who has transformed personal tragedy into a mission to save as many people as possible from her sister's fate, and <a style="text-decoration: none; color: #0d2c6c;" href="https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fnews%2f2017%2falreza-ahadi-against-all-odds&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066">Alireza Ahadi</a> shares how he's harnessed personal inspiration and big data skills to develop new tools for targeted treatments of disease.</p>
<p>Engineering alumnus <a style="text-decoration: none; color: #0d2c6c;" href="https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fconnect%2falumni-profiles%2fgreg-mctaggart&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066">Greg McTaggart</a> also shares his passion for sustainability and mega events in a career encompassing the 2000 Sydney Olympics and most recently, as the Director of Building for the iconic Sydney Opera House.</p>
<p>For those thinking of further study, <a style="text-decoration: none; color: #0d2c6c;" href="https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.uts.edu.au%2ffuture-students%2fpostgraduate&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066">applications for postgraduate enrolments are now open for second semester</a>, with <a style="text-decoration: none; color: #0d2c6c;" href="https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.alumni.uts.edu.au%2fadvantage&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066">UTS alumni automatically entitled to a 10% saving on degree courses through the UTS Alumni Advantage</a>. And applications are also open for the <a style="text-decoration: none; color: #0d2c6c;" href="https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.uts.edu.au%2fresearch-and-teaching%2ffuture-researchers%2ffuture-research-staff%2fchancellors-postdoctoral-research-0-0&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066">UTS Chancellor's Postdoctoral Research Fellowship</a>, closing Friday 19 May.</p>
<p>Once again, congratulations to our newest <a style="text-decoration: none; color: #0d2c6c;" href="https://www.alumni.uts.edu.au/page.redir?target=https%3a%2f%2fwww.instagram.com%2fexplore%2ftags%2futsalumni%2f&srcid=49165&srctid=1&erid=11685052&trid=dd453f96-bd25-4ec5-9bad-5519bee33066">#utsalumni</a>.</p>
<p>Kind regards,</p>
<p><strong>Kristy White</strong> <br /> Deputy Director, Alumni Relations<br /> UTS Advancement</p>
